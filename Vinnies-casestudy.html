VINNIES Shop

Vinnies Shops provide furniture, clothing and household goods to families and people who are in need or being assisted by conference members. The excess is provided for sale to the public.

Vinnies Shops needed

  • A mobile app for customers to place orders
  • A web for the St Vincent de paul organisition that creates Vinnies shop to receive support from donors

The Problem

The impact of pendamnic discourage users to shop in physical store and the Vinnie’s needs to have their own online shop to improve thier customer shopping expirience as well as its competitive advantage.

Research

Interview

We conducted four interviews with potential to find out user needs and thier thought of current website of St Vincent, Vinnies ebay and online shopping experince.

  • Q1 : When you go to online shopping app, what are the first things you’re likely to check?
  • Q2 : Do you feel comfortable when shopping with Vinnies Ebay Shop? Why?
  • Q3 : Is there any hurdle when you Visit St Vincent de paul landing page (minor/major)?

All interview participants mentioned few key features that they would check first; a product price, a good picture quality, description and delivery waiting time

All participants mentioned about the issue betweenprice and quality. Need encourage to shop and unclear aboutdeliver time and method.

80% users failed to navigate to vennies ebay shop and gave up because of frustration. They also mention the content information on its landing page was a bit clutted and no sense hierarc

The Solution

  • A mobile first design online shop with clearer categories and pick up options for users who didn’t like long delivery time.
  • Implimenting a clearner nevigation path for St Vincent de paul landing page
